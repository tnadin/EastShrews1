About Us

East Shrewsbury BRC was established in 8888 and having been racing 1/8th and 1/10th nitro/electric buggies for a number of years, a friendly comunity based in Hadnal, Shrewsbury. Race events can be found on the on the calender page.

What is Required to Race

There are a few key items that are required to join the racing comunity

  • BRCA Licence
  • 1/8 Buggy/Truggy
  • .21 Nitro Engine or Motor that conforms with the BRCA handbook
  • Personal Transponder
